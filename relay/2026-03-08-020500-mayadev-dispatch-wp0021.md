### Briefing

Literature survey of human journeys to the Nanga Parbat massif across
~10,000 years. Five parts: ancient travellers (pilgrims, Silk Road
merchants), medieval (Arab, Mughal, Sikh/Dogra), colonial &
mountaineering (Mummery through Buhl), modern (Messner to dam
workers), and voices never heard (women, porters, love stories).
