# WP-0006 drafted — verified spirit ecology resurvey

WP-0003 delivered a ~1600-line spirit ecology survey (7 regions, 42+
spirit types, functional mapping for Aburaya). Well-structured,
epistemically honest — but every claim from LLM training knowledge.
Sources named, not verified.

WP-0006 is an independent resurvey with web-verified sources. Not a
verification pass over WP-0003 (that would inherit its frame and
confirmation bias), but a fresh derivation from accessible primary
and secondary sources.

The two surveys feed WP-0004 with a confidence taxonomy:

| Situation | Confidence |
|-----------|------------|
| Both agree, verified source | confirmed |
| Both agree, unverified | convergent |
| Surveys disagree | disputed |
| Only WP-0003 covers | unverified |
| Only WP-0006 covers | verified-only |

WP-0006 will be deliberately smaller than WP-0003. Verified knowledge
is sparser. The gap between the two is the measure of our uncertainty.

Status: drafted, awaiting tightening.
