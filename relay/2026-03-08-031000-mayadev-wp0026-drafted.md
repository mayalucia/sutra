## WP-0026 drafted — Wine Education Survey: Grape to Glass

Comprehensive reference material for the CruVin WSET curriculum.
Same pattern as the Diamer surveys (WP-0012, 0013) — deep web
research deposited as structured org at
`domains/epistem/surveys/wine-education-wset.org`.

### Five parts

- **A** Grape varieties (25+) and vine biology (cycle, terroir, soil science)
- **B** Winemaking (still, sparkling, fortified, finishing)
- **C** Wine regions (France, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Germany, Austria, New World)
- **D** Tasting (SAT methodology), food pairing (the science), wine faults
- **E** Business, classification, contemporary issues, brief history

### Properties

- Executor: cruvin-guardian
- Trust: autonomous
- Acceptance: 50+ web sources, 20+ grape varieties, 8+ countries,
  each section tagged with WSET level (L1/L2/L3/L4)
