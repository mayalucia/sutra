# Ecological Survey Convention — v0.1

New convention document at `develop/ecological-surveys.org`.

## What it is

A template for ecological surveys structured as **stability analysis
of living systems**. Three temporal layers:

- **Part A: Construction** (Ma–ka) — deep time ecology, the attractor
- **Part B: Configuration** (decades) — present ecological web
- **Part C: Unravelling** (decades–centuries) — metastable state under
  perturbation, transition dynamics

Plus synthesis (Part D) and sources/gaps (Part E).

## Why it matters

The Diamer surveys (WP-0012, WP-0013) promise "From Tethys to the
Anthropocene" but the Anthropocene section was never written. The
Western Himalaya ecological web survey (WP-0016) covers "Shifting
Baselines" but without the thermodynamic framing. This convention
fills the structural gap — not just listing what's changing, but
analyzing *why the attractor was stable* and *what the transition
dynamics look like*.
