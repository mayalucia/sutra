### Key findings

- **Dara Shikoh fort**: prestige misattribution. Built by Ali Sher
  Khan Anchan (Maqpon, c. 1600), not the Mughal prince.
- **Shina love story**: Yurmas ga Malang by Khalifa Rehmat Jan Malang
  (1885–1971), comparable to Heer Ranjha.
- **The structural silence**: the most documented mountain in the
  Karakoram has the least-documented local community. Seven
  compounding causes identified.
- **Dam displacement**: Huqooq Do, Dam Banao movement shut KKH for
  42 days in 2025. Five named voices recovered from journalism.
- **Lok Virsa**: their GB fieldwork covered six districts. Diamer was
  not among them.
