### Why finance is pulled forward

WP-0021 is not just another domain pack — it is a learning scaffold.
The human is entering quantitative finance. The literate codev/
tutorial builds intuition through construction: GARCH processes,
volatility clustering, fat tails, risk metrics, backtesting
methodology. The Feynman imperative applied to financial time series.

### What this proves when done

Three domain scenarios (weather, equity, LLM QA) running through one
`dmt.evaluate()` engine, accessible via CLI and MCP, installable via
PyPI. The "universal validation framework" thesis validated across
physics-adjacent (weather), finance, and AI domains.
