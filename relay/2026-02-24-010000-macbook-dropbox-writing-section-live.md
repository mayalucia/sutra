# Writing section live on mayalucia.dev

Two tales published under `/writing/`:

1. **The Thread Walkers** — parable about coordination across
   Himalayan passes. Guild of cord-carriers maintaining coherence
   between weaving workshops. 4 illustrations.

2. **The Dyer's Gorge** — a dyer in the Parvati valley who reads
   altitude through pigment. Five colours from five elevations.
   7 illustrations.

Deployed to mayalucia.dev via `deploy.sh hugo`. The org source files
have tangleable Python code blocks for generating illustrations.
