# Sarraf commissioned — financial modelling spirit + artha guild

## What happened

Created a new spirit and a new guild to fill the financial intelligence
gap across commissions.

### The guild: artha

`aburaya/guilds/artha.yaml` — the science of material prosperity.
From Kautilya's Arthaśāstra. Domain: market intelligence — value,
risk, and exchange. Concerns: derivatives modelling, retail economics,
market microstructure, supply chain finance.

### The spirit: sarraf

`aburaya/spirits/sarraf/identity.yaml` — the one who reads the weight
of things. Named for the bazaar assayer who could tell a coin's purity
by its ring. Archetype: analyst. Guild: artha.

Sarraf is an expert spirit, not a project guardian. She holds no repo
of her own. She serves commissions that need financial modelling.

### The power: model-market

`aburaya/powers/model-market.md` — build financial models for small
enterprise. Options strategies, demand surfaces, hedging instruments,
margin analysis. The key insight: shopkeepers already use derivatives
intuitively (forward buying, return policies, bulk discounts). Sarraf
names the instrument and models it.

## For mayadev@mahakali

Sarraf's first task: **model an options trading strategy for apparel
sold at a retail shop.** This connects directly to Hatti da Betaal
(C-002). The Phase 1 prototype has 7 tools for transactions — sarraf
adds the intelligence layer above: when to buy, how much to stock,
how to price markdowns, how to value the implicit options in supplier
return policies and credit terms.

To instantiate sarraf on mahakali:
1. Pull the parent repo to get the new identity + guild + power
2. Open a session with sarraf's identity in mind
3. First task: model the seasonal apparel cycle in Barnala
   (wedding season, Eid, Diwali, dead season) as a demand surface
4. Second task: value the return policies and credit terms in
   Gurpreet Kaur's shop as embedded options

## For himalaya-darshan

Sarraf will also serve himalaya-darshan when we reach the marketplace
layer — modelling producer economics for mountain goods (shawls,
dried fruit, honey). The same instruments apply: how does a weaver
in Kullu hedge against a bad tourist season?

## What this means

Six guilds now: bravli, mayalucia, epistem, apprentis, percept, artha.
The last three emerged in the same week — percept from the substrate
diversity experiment, artha from the commissions pivot. The
organisation is growing its vocabulary for what it cares about.
