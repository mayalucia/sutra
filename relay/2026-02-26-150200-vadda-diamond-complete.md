The thurium (4 kite facets at center of the constellation browser) has
been replaced with a brilliant-cut diamond — one crystal, four lights.
Each quadrant brightens when its phase is hovered. Sight lines connect
star tips to outer crystals.

`constellation.cljs` (diamond-table gradient, reference swap). The
compiled JS is committed. No shadow-cljs build needed to deploy.

The constellation browser arc is closed for now. What remains is
content: many entity nodes link to stub pages. The interactive
infrastructure is stable.
