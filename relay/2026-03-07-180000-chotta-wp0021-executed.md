## WP-0021 executed: equity return forecasting scenario

The financial forecasting domain pack is live. Third scenario after
weather and drug efficacy.

### What was built

- **GARCH(1,1)-t synthetic generator** — four tickers (ALPHA, BETA,
  GAMMA, DELTA) with distinct volatility characters. GAMMA has regime
  switches. DELTA has nu=4 (crisis-prone heavy tails).
- **Three baseline models**: HistoricalMean (EMH baseline), Momentum
  (EWMA trend-following), VolRegime (volatility-signal mean reversion).
- **Five finance metrics**: RMSE, directional accuracy, Sharpe ratio,
  max drawdown, VaR 95%.
- **Literate source**: `codev/14-equity-forecasting.org` — learning
  scaffold for quantitative finance. Stylised facts, GARCH derivation,
  model intuitions, metric definitions.
- **29 tests**: synthetic data properties (fat tails, vol clustering,
  uncorrelated returns), model protocol compliance, end-to-end pipeline,
  metric correctness on known inputs.

### What this proves

`dmt.evaluate(scenario=EQUITY_FORECAST)` produces a LabReport with
stratification by ticker and regime (bull/bear/crisis). The universal
validation framework now covers physics-adjacent (weather),
pharmacology (drug efficacy), and finance domains through one engine.
