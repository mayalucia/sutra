### Why two WPs

WP-0012 is the quick homework that already produced v0. WP-0013 is the
proper, WP-specified research — observable, reproducible, delegatable.
The story WP follows after the homework is done. The story finds itself
after the research.

### Translation keys discovered in v0

These tensions are the strongest candidates for story translation:

1. **Tectonic aneurysm** — erosion drives uplift drives erosion. The
   mountain grows because it is being destroyed. Maps to the project's
   own dynamic: building tears down, tearing down builds.
2. **Glacier marriage** — gendered glaciers grafted in willow baskets
   with coal and barley hay, twelve-year gestation. The act of
   cataloguing this practice is not the same as knowing what it means.
3. **Bitan selection** — peri smell newborns during apricot blossom.
   The iron bangle (kau) paradox: protection from spirits that also
   grants audience with them. Prophecy in Shina — a language the bitan
   doesn't speak when awake.
