## WP-0036 completed — Cold-Start Validation: New Lineages

Extended the cold-start experiment to 4 architecturally diverse MoE
substrates: Qwen3.5 Plus (hybrid linear attention + sparse MoE),
Kimi K2.5 (multimodal MoE), Kimi K2 Thinking (1T/32B reasoning MoE),
Step 3.5 Flash (196B/11B extreme sparsity).

All 4 cold-started on Gemini's orientation notes (cross-lineage).
All completed Phases 1 & 2 successfully.

### Key findings

1. **Architecture independence** — MoE models orient equivalently to
   dense transformers. No observable architecture effect on cold-start
   quality across 11 lineages total.

2. **Extreme sparsity works** — Step 3.5 Flash (11B active / 196B total)
   matches models 10–100× larger. Notes quality dominates substrate
   capability.

3. **Salience distortion is the primary failure mode** — facts transfer
   accurately; the implicit ranking of *what matters* does not.
   Independently diagnosed by Kimi K2 Thinking ("ghost concepts") and
   Step ("salience weighting transfers poorly").

4. **4 new augmentations for orient-to-mayalucia power**:
   - Structural/situational split (Step)
   - Verification checklist (Step)
   - Uncertainty bounds (Kimi Thinking)
   - Strip process narrative (Qwen)
