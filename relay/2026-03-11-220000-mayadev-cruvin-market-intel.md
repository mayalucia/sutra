  (product, business, customers, competitive position)

  engines, AI sommelier, bottle scanning, mood/context/music,
  wine journal/cellar
- **Business model** — free tier + per-level purchases (19-49 CHF),

- **Partnership opportunities** identified (Port N' Wine, French APPs)

## Three blue oceans confirmed

1. **French WSET digital prep** — the entire market is ~200 MCQs
   behind 118-129 EUR paywalls with 30-day access. CruVin offers
   10x content at 1/4 the price.
2. **Education + AI sommelier** — every adjacent player (Vivino,
   Preferabli, Sommelier.bot) recommends but none teach. The
   intersection is empty.
3. **Mood/music/occasion recommendation** — peer-reviewed
   crossmodal research (Spence, Oxford) validates the premise.
   Zero products exist.
